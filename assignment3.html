Visualization 1: Global Sales by Genre and Platform

Q1: Which genres generate the highest total global sales?

This bar chart displays the total global sales sorted by genre, highlighting which game genres generate the highest sales worldwide.

Q2: How are global sales distributed across genres for the top platforms?

This stacked bar chart shows global sales distribution by genre across the top platforms, illustrating how different genres contribute to each platform's total sales.

Visualization 2: Sales Over Time by Platform and Genre

Q1: How have global sales changed over time for the top platforms?

This line chart shows how total global video game sales have changed over time for the top five platforms. Each line represents a platform, allowing us to compare when different systems were most successful and how platform popularity rose and declined across the years.

Q2: How have global sales changed over time for the top genres?

This line chart illustrates how global sales have evolved over time for the top five genres. By comparing trends across years, the chart highlights shifts in player interest and shows which genres grew, remained stable, or declined over different periods.

Visualization 3: Regional Sales vs. Platform

Q1: How do total sales for the top platforms compare across NA, EU, JP, and Other regions?

This grouped bar chart compares total sales for the top eight platforms across North America (NA), Europe (EU), Japan (JP), and Other regions. By placing regional bars side-by-side for each platform, it becomes easy to spot which platforms perform best in specific regions.

Q2: For each top platform, which regions contribute most to its overall sales?

This stacked bar chart shows the regional sales composition for the top eight platforms. Each platform’s total sales are split into NA, EU, JP, and Other segments, highlighting regional preferences and revealing whether a platform’s success is concentrated in one region or balanced globally.
