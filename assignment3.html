Visualization 1: Global Sales by Genre and Platform

Q1: Which genres generate the highest total global sales?

The bar chart displays the total global sales sorted by genre, highlighting which game genres generate the highest sales worldwide.

Q2: How are global sales distributed across genres for the top platforms?

The stacked bar chart shows global sales distribution by genre across the top platforms, illustrating how different genres contribute to each platform's total sales.

Insight

The results suggest that overall market performance is concentrated in a small number of high-selling genres. The platform breakdown indicates that genre success is not uniform across platforms; instead, each platform exhibits a distinct sales mix.

Visualization 2: Sales Over Time by Platform and Genre

Q1: How have global sales changed over time for the top platforms?

The line chart shows how total global video game sales have changed over time for the top five platforms. Each line represents a platform, allowing us to compare when different systems were most successful and how platform popularity rose and declined across the years.

Q2: How have global sales changed over time for the top genres?

The line chart illustrates how global sales have evolved over time for the top five genres. By comparing trends across years, the chart highlights shifts in player interest and shows which genres grew, remained stable, or declined over different periods.

Insight

The time series patterns indicate that platform and genre popularity changes in waves rather than remaining stable across the full timeline. Peaks and declines likely reflect platform life cycles, technological shifts, and changing consumer preferences. Comparing multiple lines makes it possible to identify periods of market expansion or contraction, as well as moments when specific platforms or genres outperform broader trends.

Visualization 3: Regional Sales vs. Platform

Q1: How do total sales for the top platforms compare across NA, EU, JP, and Other regions?

The grouped bar chart compares total sales for the top eight platforms across North America (NA), Europe (EU), Japan (JP), and Other regions.

Q2: For each top platform, which regions contribute most to its overall sales?

This stacked bar chart shows the regional sales composition for the top eight platforms. Each platform’s total sales are split into North America (NA), Europe (EU), Japan (JP), and Other segments, highlighting regional preferences and revealing whether a platform’s success is concentrated in one region or balanced globally.

Insight

The regional comparisons reveal that platform success is geographically uneven. Some platforms appear to rely heavily on a single dominant market, while others show a more balanced distribution across regions.

Visualization 4: Nintendo vs. Other Platforms Over Time

Q1: How have global video game sales changed over time for Nintendo platforms compared to all other platforms?

This line chart compares total global sales over time between Nintendo platforms (NES, SNES, N64, GB, GBA, DS, Wii, WiiU) and all other platforms combined. It highlights when Nintendo was most dominant and how its overall sales trajectory compares to the rest of the industry across different eras.

Q2: How does Nintendo’s share of total global sales shift over time compared to the rest of the market?

This stacked area chart shows the market-wide total sales over time, split into Nintendo vs. Other platforms. The visualization reveals periods when Nintendo contributed a larger portion of total sales and periods when other platforms dominated.

Insight

The comparison suggests that Nintendo’s performance is characterized by distinct cycles of dominance associated with major hardware generations. Periods of increased Nintendo share align with platform-era peaks, while subsequent declines indicate intensified competition and market transition toward other ecosystems. Overall, the pattern emphasizes that platform leadership is dynamic and shaped by product cycles, innovation, and broader industry shifts.
